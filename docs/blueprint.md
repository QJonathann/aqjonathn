# **App Name**: Fizmat AI Tutor

## Core Features:

- Question Input Interface: A user-friendly form allowing students to input their subject, a specific concept or topic, select the desired response type (explanation or practice problem), and formulate their question.
- AI-Powered Content Generation: Utilizes a generative AI tool to process the user's input, context, and question to produce either a detailed explanation of the concept or a tailored practice problem, simulating a personal tutor.
- Dynamic Response Display: Presents the AI-generated explanation or practice problem clearly and readably on the page, ensuring complex information is easy to digest for students.

## Style Guidelines:

- The app will employ a light color scheme to maintain a professional and focused learning environment. The primary interactive color, drawing from the established brand of 'Korepetycje Online' for trust and reliability, is a bright yet steady blue (#2F7DF4). This vibrant shade encourages engagement without distraction.
- The background will feature a very light, almost white, subtle blue-gray (#ECF1F9). This choice visibly carries the hue of the primary color but is heavily desaturated and bright, providing a clean, spacious canvas that complements the educational content.
- For accentuation, a soft, complementary violet (#7A7CF0) will be used. This color is chosen for its harmonious relationship with the primary blue (approximately 30 degrees to the right on the color wheel) and provides a distinctive, yet elegant contrast to highlight key information or call-to-action elements.
- All textual content, for both headlines and body text, will use 'Inter' (sans-serif). Its neutral, modern, and highly legible design ensures excellent readability across various content lengths, which is crucial for educational material.
- Clean, minimalist vector icons will be utilized. These icons will predominantly feature academic and educational themes, with subtle, modern visual cues for AI functionality (e.g., a simplified robot head or brain icon for the assistant feature).
- The page will maintain the established website's centralized, single-column content structure for clarity. Ample whitespace will be used around text blocks and UI elements to prevent visual clutter, making the AI's responses easy to consume.
- Subtle and smooth transition animations will be used when AI responses are loaded and displayed. This will include gentle fade-ins for text and subtle loading indicators during content generation, providing a fluid and reassuring user experience.
